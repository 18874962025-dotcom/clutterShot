隐私政策

欢迎使用截了个寂寞 ClutterShot。本隐私政策说明你在使用本应用时，我们如何处理照片访问权限、本地分析结果和与你设备相关的数据。 当前版本的 ClutterShot 以本地处理为核心，不提供账号系统，不建立远程服务器存储照片，也不会将你的照片上传到云端进行分析。

生效日期：2026-04-08 当前版本：1.0.0 仅本地处理

核心说明

  • 不创建账号，不要求登录。
  • 不上传照片、缩略图或分析结果到服务器。
  • 相册扫描、重复检测、相似分组与本地分类均在设备本机完成。
  • 只有在你主动操作时，应用才会通过系统接口删除照片或同步到系统相册。

1. 我们访问哪些信息

为了完成相册整理功能，ClutterShot 可能访问以下信息：

  • 你授权访问的照片与视频资源。
  • 照片的基础元数据，例如创建时间、修改时间、分辨率、媒体类型、是否为截图、是否收藏、地理位置信息（若照片本身含有此信息）。
  • 用于本地检测的缩略图、哈希值、相似特征值和分类索引。

2. 我们如何使用这些信息

  • 识别截图并按时间分组展示。
  • 检测完全重复照片与相似照片组。
  • 生成智能命名建议和本地分类建议。
  • 显示预览图、空间释放估算和清理结果。
  • 在你确认后，通过系统相册接口执行删除或创建系统相册同步。

3. 本地处理与存储说明

ClutterShot 当前没有任何服务器保存。照片、缩略图、哈希值、相似度特征、本地分类索引和分析结果都只保存在你的设备本机，用于提升再次打开应用时的速度与体验。

这些本地数据通常存储在应用沙盒环境中，仅供本应用使用。我们不会将它们出售、共享、出租或传输给第三方。

4. 删除与系统相册同步

当你执行删除操作时，应用会调用 iOS 系统相册提供的接口完成操作。被删除的照片通常会先进入系统“最近删除”，并不一定会立即永久消失。

当你选择“同步到系统相册”时，应用会在你确认后创建或写入系统相册。该行为仅在本机生效，由你主动触发。

5. 权限说明

  • 照片权限：用于分析、预览、整理和删除你授权范围内的媒体资源。
  • 添加照片权限：用于将删除操作提交给系统相册接口，或在需要时创建系统相册并写入资源。

如果你仅授予有限照片权限，应用只能访问你选定的那部分资源。

6. 第三方服务与数据共享

根据当前版本，我们没有集成第三方广告 SDK、第三方统计 SDK，也没有提供登录、云备份或远程账号服务。

除非法律法规要求，或你主动通过系统功能将内容分享给其他应用，我们不会向第三方披露你的照片和分析数据。

7. 数据保留与删除

本地索引和分类数据会保留在你的设备上，以便提升后续扫描与浏览体验。你可以通过删除应用来移除这些应用内本地数据。

由于系统相册由 iOS 管理，已同步到系统相册或已删除到“最近删除”的照片，仍需通过系统相册进行进一步管理。

8. 未成年人隐私

本应用不面向未满 13 周岁的儿童主动收集个人信息，也不提供面向儿童的账号或社区功能。如监护人认为儿童在未经同意的情况下向本应用提供了信息，请联系我们，我们会在合理范围内协助说明。

9. 政策更新

如果未来功能发生变化，例如引入新的同步方式、账号系统或额外数据处理方式，我们会更新本隐私政策，并在新版本中发布更新后的内容。

Privacy Policy

Welcome to ClutterShot. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles photo permissions, on-device analysis results, and device-related data when you use the app. The current version of ClutterShot is built around on-device processing. It does not require accounts, does not upload your photos to a server, and does not use cloud-based photo analysis.

Effective Date: 2026-04-08 Current Version: 1.0.0 On-device Processing

Key Points

  • No account creation and no sign-in required.
  • No photo uploads, remote photo storage, or cloud-side analysis.
  • Screenshot cleanup, duplicate detection, similar grouping, and local categorization run on-device.
  • Deletion and system album sync happen only when you actively confirm those actions.

1. Information the app may access

To provide photo cleanup and organization features, ClutterShot may access:

  • Photos and videos you authorize the app to access.
  • Basic metadata such as creation date, modification date, resolution, media type, screenshot flag, favorite state, and location information if it exists on the asset.
  • Locally generated thumbnails, hashes, similarity fingerprints, and classification indexes used for on-device analysis.

2. How the information is used

  • To identify screenshots and group them by time.
  • To detect exact duplicates and visually similar photo groups.
  • To generate smart naming suggestions and local album suggestions.
  • To show previews, estimated storage savings, and cleanup results.
  • To delete selected assets or sync assets into system albums after you confirm the action.

3. On-device processing and storage

ClutterShot currently uses no server storage. Photos, thumbnails, hashes, similarity fingerprints, local album indexes, and analysis results remain on your device and are processed locally.

This local data is generally stored inside the app sandbox and is used only by ClutterShot. It is not sold, rented, shared, or transmitted to third parties by the app.

4. Deletion and sync with system albums

When you delete assets, the app uses the iOS Photos system APIs. Deleted items may first move to the Recently Deleted album rather than being removed permanently right away.

When you choose to sync to a system album, the app creates or updates that album only after your confirmation, and the action takes place locally on your device.

5. Permissions

  • Photos permission: used to analyze, preview, organize, and delete media within your authorized scope.
  • Add Photos permission: used when the app needs to perform system album changes, including deletion requests and album creation or asset insertion.

If you grant limited access, the app can only work with the selected items available to it.

6. Third-party services and data sharing

Based on the current version, ClutterShot does not integrate third-party ad SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, sign-in providers, or cloud account services.

Unless required by law, or unless you intentionally share content through system-level features, the app does not disclose your photos or analysis data to third parties.

7. Data retention and removal

Local indexes and category data may remain on your device to improve future analysis speed and browsing experience. You can remove app-local data by deleting the app.

Assets already synced to system albums or moved to Recently Deleted remain subject to Apple's Photos app behavior and should be managed there if needed.

8. Children's privacy

ClutterShot is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not provide child-oriented accounts, community features, or direct user-generated profile systems. If a guardian believes information has been provided improperly, please contact us.

9. Policy updates

If ClutterShot introduces new features in the future, such as new sync methods, account services, or additional data handling flows, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.
