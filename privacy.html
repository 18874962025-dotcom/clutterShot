Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

核心照片分析默认在你的设备上完成。

截了个寂寞 ClutterShot 主要用于本地分析截图、完全重复照片、相似照片和相册分类建议。 我们不会把你的照片、缩略图、哈希或分类索引上传到开发者服务器，但购买与地点命名增强可能会使用 Apple 提供的系统服务。

要点摘要

  • 不向开发者服务器上传照片内容
  • 照片分析、哈希和分类索引默认在本机完成
  • 购买由 Apple StoreKit 处理
  • 地点命名增强可能调用 Apple 地理服务

Core photo analysis runs on your device by default.

ClutterShot primarily analyzes screenshots, exact duplicates, similar photos, and album suggestions on-device. We do not upload your photos, thumbnails, hashes, or collection indexes to the developer's servers, while purchases and place-name enhancement may use Apple-provided system services.

At a glance

  • No photo uploads to the developer's servers
  • Analysis, hashing, and indexing run on-device by default
  • Purchases are handled by Apple StoreKit
  • Location-enhanced naming may use Apple geocoding services

1. 我们处理哪些信息

为了提供截图清理、重复照片检测、相似照片分组、本地分类和系统相册同步功能，应用可能在你的设备上处理以下信息：

  • 照片资源标识符、创建时间、修改时间、尺寸、是否截图、是否收藏、连拍标识等相册元数据
  • 图片文件大小、SHA256 哈希、dHash 指纹、相似分组标记等本地索引信息
  • 你在应用内创建的本地分类、命名建议、同步状态和手动整理结果

2. 本地处理承诺

照片预览、截图识别、完全重复判断、相似分组、空间统计和本地分类整理等核心能力默认在设备本机完成。

我们不会将照片内容、缩略图、哈希、分类索引或分析结果上传到开发者服务器。

3. 可能发生的外部服务访问

  • 内购由 Apple StoreKit 处理，用于加载商品信息、完成购买和恢复购买
  • 如果你使用地点增强命名建议，应用可能调用 Apple 的地理编码服务把照片位置坐标转换为地名

上述访问仅用于 Apple 提供的系统能力，不会把照片上传给开发者服务器。

4. 权限说明

  • 照片权限：用于读取相册内容、展示分析结果、删除照片和同步系统相册
  • 照片添加权限：用于将你确认的整理结果同步到系统相册
  • 位置相关信息不会单独请求定位权限，只会在照片本身已包含位置信息时用于地点命名增强

5. 删除与同步

  • 删除操作通过 Apple Photos 接口完成，照片通常会进入系统“最近删除”
  • 同步到系统相册会创建或更新系统相册，并把你选择的资源加入其中
  • 应用内本地分类与系统相册是两套不同层级，只有在你确认同步后才会写入系统相册

6. 本地存储与保留期限

应用会在你的设备本地存储分析索引、本地分类、命名建议状态和购买状态缓存，以便下次打开时更快展示结果。

如果你删除应用，这些本地缓存通常会随应用一起移除；系统相册中的照片和你主动同步的系统相册不会因此自动删除。

7. 我们不做的事情

  • 不建立开发者自有照片服务器
  • 不上传用户照片内容到开发者服务器
  • 不出售或出租你的照片、索引或整理结果
  • 不在未经你确认的情况下自动删除或自动同步系统相册

8. 儿童与敏感内容

本应用不以收集儿童个人信息为目的，也不提供面向儿童的社交或广告画像服务。

由于应用会处理你的相册内容，请你仅在充分理解其整理功能的情况下使用。

1. Information processed by the app

To provide screenshot cleanup, exact duplicate detection, similar photo grouping, local collections, and Photos album sync, the app may process the following data on your device:

  • Photo metadata such as local identifiers, creation time, modification time, dimensions, screenshot flags, favorites, and burst identifiers
  • Local index data such as file size, SHA256 hashes, dHash fingerprints, and similar-cluster markers
  • Local collections, suggestion states, sync states, and organization results created by you inside the app

2. On-device processing

Photo preview, screenshot detection, exact duplicate checks, similar grouping, storage estimates, and local organization are processed on-device by default for the app's core analysis flow.

We do not upload your photos, thumbnails, hashes, collection indexes, or analysis results to the developer's servers.

3. External services that may be used

  • Apple StoreKit is used for loading product information, completing purchases, and restoring purchases
  • If you use location-enhanced naming, Apple geocoding services may be used to convert existing photo coordinates into place names

These services are provided by Apple and are not used to upload your photos to the developer's servers.

4. Permissions

  • Photo access is used to read your library, show analysis results, delete assets, and sync albums
  • Photo add access is used when you choose to sync organization results back to Photos
  • The app does not request standalone location permission; it may only use location already embedded in your photos for naming enhancement

5. Deletion and sync behavior

  • Deletion is performed through Apple Photos APIs and usually moves items to Recently Deleted
  • Syncing to system albums creates or updates a Photos album and adds the assets you selected
  • Local collections and system albums are separate until you explicitly confirm syncing

6. Local storage and retention

The app stores analysis indexes, local collections, suggestion states, and purchase cache information locally on your device so results can load faster next time.

If you remove the app, that local cache is typically removed with it. Photos in your system library and albums you explicitly synced are not automatically deleted.

7. What we do not do

  • We do not operate our own photo-upload server
  • We do not upload your photo content to the developer's servers
  • We do not sell or rent your photos, indexes, or organization results
  • We do not automatically delete photos or sync system albums without your confirmation

8. Children and sensitive content

This app is not designed to collect children's personal data and does not provide ad profiling or social features for children.

Because the app works with your photo library, please use it only when you understand and accept its organization features.
